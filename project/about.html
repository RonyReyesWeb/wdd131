About the Community Hub

This platform connects Guatemalan software developers to learn, collaborate, and find opportunities. It focuses on accessible, community-driven learning and peer support.

Mission

To empower developers in Guatemala through shared resources, mentorship, and opportunities.

Vision

To build a sustainable, active network that improves skills and access to jobs and projects.

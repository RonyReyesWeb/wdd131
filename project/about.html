About the Community Hub

The Guatemalan Developer Community Hub is a digital platform created to connect software developers across Guatemala. Our goal is to provide resources, tutorials, and a space for collaboration and networking.

Developers can find learning resources, share their projects, ask questions in the forum, and explore job opportunities in the tech industry.

Mission

To empower Guatemalan developers through knowledge sharing, mentorship, and community collaboration.

Vision

To build a strong, supportive, and active network of software developers in Guatemala that fosters innovation and professional growth.
